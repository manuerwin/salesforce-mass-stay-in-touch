title Mass Stay In Touch Sequence

Salesforce User->Contact List View: Select List View

Contact List View->SendEmail VF Page: Select Contact(s) & click "Request Stay In Touch" button

note right of SendEmail VF Page
  Displayed fields determined
  by Contact Field Set
end note

alt Proceed

  SendEmail VF Page->Contact: Send SIT request email
  note left of Contact
    Email template uses Contact fields
  end note

  alt Contact chooses to update
    Contact->RespondAndSubmit Site: Choose to update Contact details
    RespondAndSubmit Site->Contact Record: Update
    Contact Record->Salesforce User: Advise change occurred (Workflow Update & Email Alert)
  else Contact chooses to confirm
    Contact->RespondAndSubmit Site: Choose to confirm Contact details (aka no change)
    RespondAndSubmit Site->Contact Record: Update
    Contact Record->Salesforce User: Advise no changes (Workflow Update & Email Alert)
  else Contact does not respond
    note over Contact,Contact: No action
  end

else Cancel
  note right of SendEmail VF Page: Click "Cancel" button
  SendEmail VF Page->Contact List View: Return
end
